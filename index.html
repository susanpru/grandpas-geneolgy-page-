The first Pruzinsky to arrive in the United States

Emery Pruzinsky first arrived at Ellis Island on May 7, 1894 at 17 years old. He came from the town of Plavnlica, a few miles from Stara Lubovna, very close to the Polish border in present Slovakia. He was born to Andrew Pruzinsky and Mary Krajnak. Within the next 10 years he was joined in the United states by his two younger brothers. They settled in Windber Pennsylvania.

Pruzinsky Family roots in Europe

The Pruzinsky name is believed to have originated in present Slovakia. There are Pruzinskys currently living in the town of Plavnica where Emery Pruzinsky was born. Located in the Tatra Mountains it is a very scenic place. There is a village in Slovenia that was named Pruzina in 1364. It got that name when it came into the property of the Pruzinsky and Lieskovsky families. It kept this name until the 1800s.
